Welcome to Cooper Mountain Ale Works Public House,
formerly Max's Fanno Creek Brew Pub!

Craft Lives Here

With Covid-19 still spreading locally, we are flexing our craft muscles to adapt and serve our community. Always committed to offering delicious food and beer, we are continually evolving our approach to keep you safe and meet current regulations. Our spacious dining room is socially distant and clean. Our outdoor patio is covered and heaters are available. We offer takeout via walk-in, phone, or online. Gift cards are available.

While our options may change, one thing remains the same: We are committed to being a place for our community to gather and celebrate craft. We have great plans for 2021.
