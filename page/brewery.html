Crafting through Art & Science

Starting off in an attached garage in Beaverton, Cooper Mountain Ale Works motto was always "Discover the beer you didn't know you loved".

What brought together the team that founded the brewery was a desire to make the beers we couldn't find locally. In the history of brewing, there have been thousands of styles created by people for centuries, and yet what you find a a lot of taprooms is variations on a small number of styles. We love what the Northwest has done to beer culture, and as we joined that community we found ourselves pulled to making things that we had a hard time finding for ourselves.

Today we brew a wide range of beer on a 10 barrel production system in downtown Tigard. We also maintain a smaller 3 barrel system, still in the garage we got our start in, for pilot batches and smaller runs of specialty beers.
